Issues Page

Unequal Resources

Many students do not have enough books, teachers, classrooms, or learning tools.

Lack of Access

Some children cannot go to school because of poverty, distance, or social problems.

Digital Gap

Not every student has internet access or digital devices for online learning.

Learning Quality

Some students attend school but still do not receive quality education.

Solutions Page

E-learning

Online learning platforms can help students learn anytime and anywhere.

Government Support

Governments can provide funding, better schools, and more teacher training.

Community Help

Organizations and volunteers can support students with resources and tutoring.

Inclusive Education

Education should be available for all students, including girls, poor children, and disabled students.
