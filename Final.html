Issues & Solutions

Understanding the barriers to education and the actions we can take to overcome them.

⚠️ The Digital Divide

Lack of stable internet and electricity leaves students in remote areas unable to access digital learning tools.

✅ Digital Infrastructure

Deploying solar-powered classrooms and offline servers to provide quality content to off-grid communities.

⚠️ High Cost of Learning

Expensive textbooks and tuition fees prevent children from low-income families from attending school.

✅ Open Resources (OER)

Providing free, openly licensed digital materials so students can learn without financial barriers.

⚠️ Shortage of Teachers

Many regions lack qualified educators, leading to overcrowded classes and low education quality.

✅ Teacher Training

Investing in professional training and fair support to empower teachers in underserved areas.
