SDG Topic:
Quality
Education

  • help users quickly understand education issues
  • improve learning opportunities
  • provide clear and simple information about education
  • create an easy-to-use and user-friendly website

Page
Layout

Home Page

Introduces SDG 4 and provides a brief overview with visuals and navigation buttons.

Issues Page

Shows global education problems, such as unequal resources and lack of access to education.

Solutions Page

Presents possible solutions, like e-learning and support from governments or organizations.

Resources Page

Provides useful links and sources for further learning.

Design
Concept

Visual Style

  • use blue and green as main color
  • use white and light gray as secondary colors
  • use simple illustrations or pictures

Design Focus

  • simplicity, clarity, readability, and accessibility
  • engaging visuals
💡

Special
Features

  • Interactive Navigation: Users can click the navigation bar to quickly move between different sections.
  • Hover Effects: Cards or images will enlarge or change color when users move the mouse over them.
  • Smooth Scrolling: When users click buttons, the page scrolls smoothly to the target section.
  • Fade-in Animation: Content gradually appears when users scroll down the page.

Creative Ideas

  • Focus on user-friendly and intuitive design
  • Use visuals to simplify complex education issues
  • Provide a clear and structured learning experience
  • Enhance engagement through interactive elements
  • Combine education with modern web design
